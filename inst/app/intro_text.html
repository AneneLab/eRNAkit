WELCOME TO eRNAkit

An interactive open source software tool for rapid analysis of eRNA functions and interactions (eRNAkit).

eRNAkit enables data driven, rapid and reproducible identifcation of eRNA-mRNA interactions with clear prediction of cytoplams and nuclear specific functions. Preprint available here: https://doi.org/XXXX.CXXX

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH eRNAkit:

  • Find eRNAs located on a particular geneomic location, and filter or stratify by various features.
  • Retrive speific eRNA ID, the associated interactions, expression and localisation. 
  • Retrive eRNAs associated with a given gene and filter by various features. 
  • Identify specific eRNA cordinates interacting with specific mRNA coordinates for AI applications,   qPC primer or siRNA design.
